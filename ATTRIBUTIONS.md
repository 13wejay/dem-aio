## 🌍 DEM Datasets & API Providers

Users downloading, visualizing, or publishing outputs derived from this application must adhere to the data usage policies and cite the original providers of the generated Digital Elevation Models.

### OpenTopography API
A significant portion of the raster data is retrieved dynamically through the **OpenTopography Global DEM API**.
*   **Attribution Policy:** "DEM Data retrieval powered by the OpenTopography Facility with support from the National Science Foundation under NSF Award Numbers 1948997, 1948994 & 1948857."

*   **Provider:** European Space Agency (ESA) & Airbus Defence and Space
*   **Licensing:** Open access, subject to the conditions of the Copernicus program.
*   **Attribution:** "© DLR e.V. (2014-2018) and © Airbus Defence and Space GmbH (2022) provided under COPERNICUS by the European Union and ESA; all rights reserved."
